```
What is the output of the following code? 
 
 LocalDate date = LocalDate.of(2018, Month.APRIL, 30).plusMonths(-1) 
                           .plusYears(20); 
 System.out.println(date.getYear() + " " + date.getMonth() + " " 
                    + date.getDayOfMonth()); 
   
```


- 1 2018 MARCH 30
- 2 2018 APRIL 30
- 3 2018 MAY 30
- 4 2038 MARCH 30
- 5 2038 APRIL 30
- 6 2038 MAY 30

Answer: *4*
